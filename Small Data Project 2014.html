I'm also curious about April/May being so well represented in terms of stuff I did. It was raining a lot then, so I probably spent quite a bit of time inside. Apparently writing and doing music.

Ultimately, my biggest question is whether the project was a success: did I do at least three things more than I didn't?

To figure that out, I calculate the cumulative sum of activities done vs. laziness. I felt like it was necessary to reweight my laziness. if I didn't do something during the day, I wasn't 3x more lazy than when I wasn't doing something.

activities = np.cumsum(df.active_sum)
activities_diff = np.cumsum(df.active_sum.diff())
laziness = np.cumsum(df.D) / 3

Taking into account scaling, I did a pretty good job of getting things done, but I would have liked to be more consistently productive toward the end of the year.
